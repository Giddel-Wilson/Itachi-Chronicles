Second, he wanted to keep an eye on the Akatsuki and make sure that they did not pose a threat to Konohagakure. Third, he wanted to protect his younger brother, Sasuke, from the Akatsuki.

Itachi's decision to join the Akatsuki was a controversial one. Many people believed that he had betrayed Konohagakure and that he was now a villain. However, Itachi's true intentions were never malicious. He simply wanted to protect the people he loved and to create a better world.
